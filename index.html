Our story
Born in Gurugram,
Built by hand
Masale Arpan started with a simple belief — real spices shouldn't have shortcuts. We set up right here in Laxman Vihar, Gurugram, built our own grinding machine from scratch, and got FSSAI certified to do this properly.

We source raw spices directly from farms in Rajasthan, Kerala and Kashmir. They're cleaned, cold-pounded in small batches, and packed fresh — no fillers, no artificial colour, no preservatives. When you open a pack, you'll know the difference.
